Badminton

Badminton is a sport similiar to tennis, with a racket and a birdie, or something you hit. The point of badminton is to hit the birdie onto the other side of the net and at the same time keeping the the birdie inside the court. You win the game if you score 21 points by hitting the birdie onto the floor of the other side, over the net, and inside the court. You win the game if you win 2 rounds, solo or doubles.



Starcraft

Starcraft is a game involving 3 races fighting for dominance. The three races include monsters, aka zerg, aliens, aka protoss, and humans, aka terrans. The actual gameplay consists of building and expanding your base while simultaneously scouting the enemie'(s) to find their locations and figuring out their gameplay, building a large army to counter the opponent'(s), splitiing up your army to attack and defend at the same time, etc.
