Step 4- Transfering Tariables

Transfering variables is fairly easy. Start by writing var in the JS followed by the name of your div in HTML. Next, add "document.getElementById" which would find and bring the div from HTML to JS. Follow the "document.getElementById" with (name of you variable in HTML). Now, all of your divs are transfered to JS.
